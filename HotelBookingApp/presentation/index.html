Problem Statement & Solution
⚠ The Problem
Fragmented Hotel Operations
  • ✕No unified platform — guests search, book & pay across disconnected tools
  • ✕Hotel admins manage rooms, rates & inventory in spreadsheets
  • ✕No real-time availability — overbooking & manual errors are common
  • ✕Refund & cancellation processes are slow and fully manual
  • ✕No platform-level oversight for multi-hotel management
  • ✕Guests have no self-service support or AI assistance
✓ Our Solution
One Platform, Three Roles
  • ✓Guests search, book, pay & cancel — all in one seamless flow
  • ✓Admins get a full dashboard: rooms, rates, inventory, reservations
  • ✓Real-time per-date inventory — zero overbooking guaranteed
  • ✓Automated refunds via background services on cancellation
  • ✓SuperAdmin controls all hotels, amenities & platform revenue
  • ✓AI chatbot (Groq LLaMA 3.1) for instant role-aware guest support
