help_outline Quick FAQ

How do I cancel a booking?

Go to My Bookings, open the reservation, and click Cancel Reservation. If you paid, a refund request is created automatically.

When will I receive my refund?

Direct refunds (within 30 min of payment) are instant. Admin-approved refunds are processed within 3–5 business days.

How do I list my hotel?

Register as a Hotel Admin at Register Your Property. Setup takes under 5 minutes.

My hotel is showing as blocked. What do I do?

Contact our platform support at thanush@superadmin.com and we will review your case.
